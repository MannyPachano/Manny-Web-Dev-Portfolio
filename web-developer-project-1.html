Project Overview

nuVector, parent company of the LoopManager Supply Chain Management App, embarked on a website revamp ahead of a major marketing push. Encouraged by positive feedback on their new animated explainer videos, they wanted to align their website with the videos' tone and style for consistent branding. They envisioned a modern, visually appealing site with captivating graphics.


Beginning with lofi wireframes to map out user journeys, we progressed to high fidelity mockups and prototypes using Figma's collaborative features to keep everyone in the loop, no pun intended. Opting for a 100% code-based approach instead of WordPress plugins enhanced site speed and allowed for tailored backend processes, also optimizing for long-term SEO initiatives..


Leveraging HTML, CSS, JS, and PHP, I crafted a secure, dynamic frontend and rewrote the copy for improved clarity and engagement. The result exceeded expectations, delighting nuVector with its polished appearance and enhanced functionality.
